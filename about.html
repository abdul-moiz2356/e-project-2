About Us

CARVAN

Since 1998, carvan Auto Dealership has been serving our community with integrity, transparency, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive destinations.

Our founder, Moiz Khan, had a simple vision: to create a car buying experience that puts customers first. Today, that vision continues to drive everything we do, from our carefully curated inventory to our no-pressure sales approach.

We believe that buying a car should be exciting, not stressful. That's why we've built our reputation on honest dealings, competitive pricing, and exceptional after-sales service.
